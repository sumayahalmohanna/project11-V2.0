Learn how

Synergics has proven methods for accelerating the successful development & commercialization of new businesses, technologies and other solutions.

These methods are based on:

  • Conversion of technology-product-opportunity push into market pull.
  • Maintenance of client anonymity for as long as appropriate.
  • Early simultaneous, private, confidential engagement-collaboration with a client's best external resources with and through whom implementation of business development is more rapidly and successfully accomplished.
  • External resources that include upper-level decision-making top-management (CXOs/others), specialty knowledge experts and authorities, customers, technology suppliers... critical stakeholders.
  • Intense private, confidential face-to-face interaction amongst critical stakeholders on exactly how our anonymous client can realistically achieve business success (using our Summit Business Development System™)

Benefits to clients

  • Anonymously penetrate and understand the mindset of a marketplace or industry sector.
  • Secure early indication of industry stakeholder interest in client project or opportunity.
  • Eliminate bias by keeping the client anonymous.
  • Secure real world stakeholder feedback (before generating third party feasibility studies or detailed business plans) about fit, utility, value, return, risk, other insights on being successful from the industry leaders (prospective customers, suppliers and other partners with and through whom implementation-commercialization will be more rapid and successful).
  • Achieve market pull through real time market-technology validation (a collective market-technology due diligence).
  • Reduce time to market and sales/commercialization.
  • Produce needed bankable agreements.
  • Minimize risks and maximize opportunities.
  • Avoid “loose ends”.
